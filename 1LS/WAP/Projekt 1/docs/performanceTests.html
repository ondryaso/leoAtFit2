performanceTests

Generates very long object chains of objects with large number of random properties, runs the implementations and measures the time they take, then compares whether the results are the same.

Before comparing the results, the generated values are collected in arrays and sorted This is done to ignore differences in the order of returned names (as no particular ordering of the names yielded by the generator is required by the specification).

The number of properties and objects in a chain may be controlled by changing the numProperties and numObjectsInChain constants.
